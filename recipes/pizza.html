Pizza

Description

An easy and delicious pizza with pre-baked dough!

Ingredients

  • Pre-baked dough
  • Tomato-sauce
  • 3 Tomatoes
  • 1 cup of spinach
  • Grated Cheese
  • Seasonings like salt, pepper, oregano

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 200 degrees celcius.
  2. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the pre-baked dough.
  3. Cut the tomatoes into small pieces and distribute evenly over the dough.
  4. Season with Salt, Pepper and Oregano.
  5. Cover with the grated cheese.
  6. Bake for 5-6 Minutes, put Spinach on the Pizza after around 4 Minutes.
